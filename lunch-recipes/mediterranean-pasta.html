Mediterranean Pasta

Servings: 4

Total time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 300g pasta shells
  • Finely grated rind and juice of 1 lemon
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 75g chorizo (a third of a ring), skin removed and sliced into thin semi-circles
  • 1 large red onion, sliced
  • 2 small peppers, cut into chunks
  • 200g baby plum tomatoes, quartered
  • 60g black olives, quartered
  • 75g sundried tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • 120g feta cheese, drained and cut into small cubes
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh mint

Method

  1. Cook the pasta according to the pack instructions. Once cooked and drained, stir in the mayonnaise, lemon juice and lemon rind, then season with plenty of black pepper.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, in a frying pan, gently heat the chorizo until it starts to release its oils.
  3. Add the onion and pepper, then gently fry for 10 minutes until the onion is softened.
  4. Stir the chorizo mixture into the pasta with the remaining ingredients. Season to taste.
