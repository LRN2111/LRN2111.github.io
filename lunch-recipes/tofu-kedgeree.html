Tofu Kedgeree

Servings: 4

Total time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 260g basmati rice
  • 1 tbsp olice oil
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 300g marinated tofu pieces or frozen vegetarian chicken-style pieces
  • 1 head of broccoli, cut into small florets
  • 200g frozen peas
  • 3 tbsp medium curry powder
  • ½ tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 tsp mustard seeds
  • Soy sauce, to taste
  • 1 bunch spring onions, sliced

Method

  1. In a medium saucepan, boil the rice and eggs. Remove the eggs after 7 minutes and place in a bowl of ice water, but leave the rice for a total of 10 minutes. Drain.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the oil in a large, deep pan and soften the onion for 5 minutes.
  3. Add the broccoli and peas and fry for a further 3 minutes. If using frozen chicken-style pieces, add these at the same time.
  4. Add all the spices and fry for 1-2 minutes more.
  5. Stir the rice into the vegetables with a splash of water (and the tofu, if using this). Season with black pepper and soy sauce, then heat gently through for a few minutes until everything is cooked. Stir the spring onions in at the very end.
  6. Peel and quarter the boiled eggs, then serve the rice, topped with the quartered eggs.
