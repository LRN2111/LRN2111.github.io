Bean and Chorizo Chilli

Servings: 2

Total time: ? minutes

Ingredients

  • 100g chorizo, sliced
  • 1 large red onion, sliced
  • 1 stick of celery, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • ½ tsp mild chilli powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 250g passata
  • 1 red pepper, chopped
  • 1 tin kidney beans in chilli sauce
  • 1 tin black beans, drained and rinsed

Method

  1. Dry fry the chorizo until it starts to release its oil.
  2. Add the onion, celery and garlic, and fry for 5 minutes until soft.
  3. Add the spices and fry for a further minute.
  4. Add the passata. Stir, cover and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Add the pepper, kidney beans and black beans. Simmer with the lid on for a further 10 minutes.
  6. Season to taste. Serve with rice.
