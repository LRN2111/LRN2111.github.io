Creamy Mushroom Gnocchi

Servings: 2

Total time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 5 small shallots or 1 onion, finely sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 250g chestnut mushrooms, thickly sliced
  • 500g ready-made gnocchi
  • 125ml white wine
  • 125ml vegetable stock
  • Large handful baby spinach
  • 3 tbsp half-fat crème fraîche
  • Grana Padano cheese (or other vegetarian hard cheese)

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a large, lidded frying pan or casserole dish, and gently fry the shallots/onion and garlic for 2-3 minutes until starting to soften.
  2. Add the mushrooms to the pan, turn up the heat and cook for 3-4 minutes. Season well with salt and black pepper.
  3. Add the white wine, let it bubble for a minute, the add the vegetable stock and gnocchi. Turn down to a medium simmer, cover with a lid and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Tear in the spinach and stir in the crème fraîche. Taste and adjust the seasoning, then serve with a grating of cheese.
