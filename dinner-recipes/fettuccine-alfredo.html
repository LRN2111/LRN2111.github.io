Fettuccine Alfredo

Servings: 2

Total time:

Ingredients

  • ½ tbsp olive oil
  • 2 shallots or 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 50g pancetta or bacon lardons
  • 1 clove of garlic, crushed
  • 200g chestnut mushrooms - half finely chopped, half thickly sliced
  • 300g skinless and boneless chicken thighs
  • ½ tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 50ml chicken stock
  • 75g half-fat crème fraîche
  • 150g fettuccine or tagliatelle
  • Finely grated zest and juice of half a lemon
  • Small handful fresh tarragon, leaves picked and roughly chopped

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a large frying pan and gently fry the shallots and pancetta for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the garlic and mushrooms and fry for a further 2-3 minutes.
  3. Turn up the heat and add the chicken. Fry on a medium-high heat for 5-6 minutes or until the chicken is almost cooked through.
  4. Stir in the mustard and the stock. Simmer and reduce for 1-2 minutes then stir in the crème fraîche.
  5. Reduce the heat and simmer for 10 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has reduced.
  6. Meanwhile, cook the pasta according to the pack instructions, then drain.
  7. Stir the lemon zest, lemon juice and tarragon into the sauce, then taste and season. Stir the drained pasta into the sauce, then serve, garnished with extra chopped tarragon.
