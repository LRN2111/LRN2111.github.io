Leek and Mackerel Pasta Bake

Servings: 2

Total time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 150g pasta shapes e.g. macaroni
  • 2 leeks, thinly sliced
  • 1 small head of broccoli, cut into small florets
  • 2 smoked peppered mackerel fillets, skin removed
  • 17g butter
  • 17g plain flour
  • 150ml milk
  • 60g cheddar
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 25g chives, snipped
  • 25g breadcrumbs (optional)

Method

  1. In a medium saucepan, cook the pasta according to pack instructions. Add the leeks after 2 minutes and the broccoli after 4 minutes.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, flake the mackerel into an ovenproof dish.
  3. Drain the pasta and vegetables and pour into the dish with the mackerel.
  4. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C (fan).
  5. Next, make the cheese sauce. Melt the butter in the saucepan then add the flour. Stir for 2 minutes. Gradually whisk in the milk, then add the mustard and half the cheddar. Continue whisking until the sauce is thick, then season to taste.
  6. Pour the cheese sauce over the pasta, add the chopped chives, then stir everything together.
  7. Mix the remaining cheddar with the breadcrumbs (if using), then sprinkle over the pasta. Bake for 15 minutes, until golden brown.
