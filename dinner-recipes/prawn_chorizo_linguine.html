Prawn and Chorizo Linguine

Servings: 2

Total time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 50g chorizo, sliced
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary, leaves finely chopped
  • 1 clove of garlic, crushed
  • ½ red chilli, deseeded and finely chopped
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds
  • 400g passata
  • 75ml white wine
  • ½ tbsp tomato purée
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 150 linguine
  • 150g cooked king prawns
  • 1 tsp caster sugar

Method

  1. In a large pan, fry the chorizo, rosemary, garlic, chilli and fennel seeds with a little olive oil for 2 minutes.
  2. Add the passata, wine, tomato purée and oregano. Increase the heat and simmer for about 10 minutes to thicken the sauce, stirring occasionally.
  3. Halfway through, cook the linguine.
  4. Add the prawns to the sauce to warm through for 3-4 minutes. Taste, then season and add the sugar if it tastes too sharp.
  5. Drain the cooked pasta, then stir in the finished sauce.
