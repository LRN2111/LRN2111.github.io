Slow Cooker BBQ Ribs

Servings: 2

Total time: 4 ½ hours

Ingredients

  • 700g pork ribs
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp mild chilli powder
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 pork stock cube
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 cloves of garlic, peeled and left whole
  • ½ tsp mustard seeds
  • ½ tsp peppercorns
  • 100g BBQ sauce

Method

  1. Mix the dry spices together with plenty of salt and pepper. Rub the ribs with the spice mix and leave to marinate for at least an hour, ideally a few or overnight.
  2. Place the ribs into the slow cooker and add the pork stock cube, bay leaf, garlic, mustard seeds and peppercorns. Add enough boiling water to just cover the ribs, then cook on high for 3 hours 45 minutes.
  3. Towards the end of the cooking time, preheat the oven to 200Â°C (fan) and line a roasting tin with foil.
  4. Carefully remove the ribs from the slow cooker using tongs and place into the roasting tin. Brush liberally with BBQ sauce.
  5. Roast in the oven for 20 minutes, turning halfway.
