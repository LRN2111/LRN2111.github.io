Chilli Con Carne

Servings: 4

Total time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 500g lean minced beef
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 1 tsp mild chilli powder OR 1 finely chopped chilli
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • ½ tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 tin (400g) chopped tomatoes
  • 1 red pepper, chopped
  • 1 tin kidney beans in chilli sauce
  • 1 tbsp beef gravy granules (optional)

Method

  1. In a large saucepan, fry the beef, onion and garlic until the beef is browned. Break the beed up with a wooden spoon as it cooks.
  2. Drain any excess fat into a disposable container.
  3. Add the spices and fry for a further minute.
  4. Add the tomato puree and beef stock cube, then pour in enough boiling water to just cover everything. Stir, cover and simmer pn a low heat for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Add the pepper, kidney beans and chopped tomatoes. Simmer with the lid off for a further 15 minutes.
  6. Season to taste, and add the beef gravy granules if the sauce needs thickening. Serve with rice, pasta or jacket potatoes.
