Double Chocolate Chip Muffins

Servings: 12

Total time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 280g plain flour
  • 75g cocoa powder
  • 2½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 250g granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300ml buttermilk
  • 55g unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  • 60ml vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp instant coffee
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 170g chocolate chunks

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C fan and line a 12 cup muffin tin with muffin cases.
  2. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda and salt. Add the sugar and mix.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs with the buttermilk, oil, melted butter, coffee granules and vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients along with the chocolate chunks, then stir until just combined. Do not overmix - the batter should be thick and lumpy.
  5. Divide the mixture between the muffin cases, filling almost all the way to the top.
  6. Bake for 3 minutes, then reduce the temperature to 160°C. Continue to bake for 12-17 minutes (15-20 minutes total) until a toothpick inserted into the centre comes out clean. Transfer to a wire rack and cool for 10 minutes before removing from the pan.
